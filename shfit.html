Shfit App

While flexibility in scheduling and work hours is growing more popular in the workspace, swapping shifts amongst employees can be a disorganized process. Shfit addresses the issues of matching skill level, communication, and confusion in scheduling and exchanging shifts amongst gym employees in one virtual space.

Introduction

Our team (Course: Interaction Design Overview, HCII CMU) was tasked to design a native mobile app in a funnel design process to allow for better shift swapping amongst employees.

My Role

I worked with a team on all parts of the design process. Specifically, I worked on primary and secondary research and interviews to discover issues- some on the employee side and all for the admin/manager side of scheduling and shift swapping, helped with the customer journey map and created a manager persona, translated manager-side issues into UX solutions, created wireframes for messaging/notifying and the flowchart, refined the final stylized screens for messaging and chatting (and helped refine others), worked on inVision to prototype, helped with user testing, worked with my team to address and resolve conceptual issues in our iterations, and with delivery of the final pitch.
